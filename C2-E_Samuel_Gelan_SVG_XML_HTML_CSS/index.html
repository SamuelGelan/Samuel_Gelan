You're going to fall in love with Fasil Ghebbi

The Fasil Ghebbi is a fortress located in Gonder, Amhara Region; it was founded in the 17th century by Emperor Fassilides and was the home of Ethiopian Emperors since then. Fascinatingly, most of the monuments have preserved their authenticity and remain in a n overall good state of conservation. The fortress city functioned as the center of Ethiopian goverment until 1864.

The mysterious rock-hewn church in Lalibela, Ethiopia

If faith is a mystery, there are few place in the chrstian world where the mystery is deeper than Lalibela. 800 years ago, an Ethiopian king ordered a new capital for christians. At 8,000 feet, on centeral plateau of Ethiopia stand 11 churches, each carved from a single, gigantic, block of stone. No bricks, no mortar no concrete, no lumber, just rock sculpted into architecture. Not much is known who built them, or why. But the faithful of the Ethipian Orthodox Church say there's no mystery really. The churches of Lalibela were built by angels.

Come to the adventures Mountains in Simien Ethiopia

Simien Mountain is an exotic setting with unique wildllife and breath-taking views on landsape shaped by nature and tradtional agriculture. The natural beauties of the region have always filled visitors from Ethiopia and abroad with awe. Gentle highland ridges at altitudes above 3600 meters above sea level, covered with grasses, isolated trees are found on the high plateau that ends abruptly at 1000-2000m deep escarpemnts.
